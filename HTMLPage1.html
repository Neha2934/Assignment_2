About Us

Chopped Leaf is a Canadian-born restaurant franchise that specializes in fresh, customizable, and flavorful meals, particularly salads, wraps, and bowls. They focus on wholesome ingredients and community connections, aiming to provide a "better-for-you" dining experience. The company is owned and managed by Innovative Food Brands and has over 100 locations in Canada and the USA, with plans for continued expansion .

Services

Chopped Leaf offers a variety of quick-service dining options, including salads, wraps, and bowls, with a focus on fresh ingredients and customizable meals. They also provide catering services for events and offer online ordering through their app. Additionally, Chopped Leaf has a rewards program where customers can earn points for discounts. .
